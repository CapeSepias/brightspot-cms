Dashboard

Once logged into Brightspot you are brought to the Dashboard. From here you are provided with widgets to find and create new content quickly. The widgets on offer can be rearranged on the page to suit your specific needs and/or preferences.

Click a widget callout to find out more.

Sitemap
View all your pages and filter by content type
Recent Activity
Recent edits and content creation
Work Streams
Manage simple tasks with work streams
Create new
Create new pages with one click
Bulk Uploader
Upload files in bulk. Drag and Drop.
Schedules
Keep track of content going live
